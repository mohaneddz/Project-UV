COLUMN             DESCRIPTION
---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Date               Observation date (YYYY-MM-DD)

--- TEMPERATURE ---
T2M                Average air temperature at 2 meters (°C)
T2MDEW             Dew/Frost point temperature at 2 meters (°C)
T2MWET             Wet bulb temperature at 2 meters (°C)
TS                 Earth skin (surface) temperature (°C)
T2M_RANGE          Diurnal temperature range (Max - Min) (°C)
T2M_MAX            Maximum daily air temperature at 2 meters (°C)
T2M_MIN            Minimum daily air temperature at 2 meters (°C)

--- HUMIDITY & PRECIPITATION ---
RH2M               Relative humidity at 2 meters (%)
PRECTOTCORR        Total precipitation (rain/snow), corrected for gauge errors (mm/day)

--- WIND (Speed & Direction) ---
WS2M  / WD2M       Wind speed (m/s) and direction (degrees) at 2 meters
WS10M / WD10M      Wind speed (m/s) and direction (degrees) at 10 meters
WS50M / WD50M      Wind speed (m/s) and direction (degrees) at 50 meters

--- SOLAR & THERMAL ---
ALLSKY_SFC_SW_DWN  Total shortwave solar energy reaching surface (All Sky) (kW-hr/m²/day)
CLRSKY_SFC_SW_DWN  Theoretical solar energy if sky were clear (no clouds) (kW-hr/m²/day)
ALLSKY_KT          Insolation Clearness Index (0.0 to 1.0; 1.0 = Clear, 0.0 = Opaque)
ALLSKY_SFC_LW_DWN  Thermal infrared energy (Longwave) emitted by atmosphere to surface

--- UV & OZONE ---
ALLSKY_SFC_UV_INDEX UV Index (0 to 11+); measure of risk of skin damage
ALLSKY_SFC_UVA      UVA Irradiance (315-400 nm); long-wave UV (aging) (W/m²)
ALLSKY_SFC_UVB      UVB Irradiance (280-315 nm); short-wave UV (burning) (W/m²)
TO3                 Total Column Ozone (Dobson Units - DU)
